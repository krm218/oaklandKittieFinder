Lost Kitties Found Kitties How does this work in 2019?

This website was created for my FEWD Project. I wanted people to be able to post whenever they lose their cat as well as whenever they find a cat.

Basically all you do is enter your cat's information and your contact information if you've lost a cat. Users will then be able to browse through and contact you if your pet is found.
